- :jsontype:`number` :jsonkey:`referenceArea` (optional). Surface area that undergoes radiation pressure. If not defined, it will be retrieved from the body's :jsonkey:`referenceArea` key to which the map of radiation pressure objects is assigned.
- :jsontype:`number` :jsonkey:`radiationPressureCoefficient` (mandatory). Radiation pressure coefficient.
